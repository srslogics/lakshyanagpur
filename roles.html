Role Views

Who Sees What

Each role has a different surface, a different action set, and a different business outcome.

Leadership

Owner / Director

Runs the institute through a high-level command view focused on growth, collections, outcomes, and risk.

Sees Revenue, admissions, branch health, occupancy, faculty load
Acts On Scholarship approvals, expansion, leadership decisions, escalations
Business Value Faster decisions and tighter institutional control
Growth

Admissions Team

Lives inside the enquiry-to-enrollment funnel and keeps every lead warm, tracked, and measurable.

Sees Lead source, parent interest, demo schedule, follow-up queue
Acts On Counselling, demo booking, pipeline movement, onboarding
Business Value Higher conversion with less lead leakage
Finance

Accounts Department

Owns fee plans, collection discipline, receipts, dues, and payment clarity across all students.

Sees Installments, dues, discounts, concessions, ageing, receipts
Acts On Collection follow-up, reconciliation, refund control, fee closure
Business Value Stronger cashflow and fewer financial blind spots
Academics

Teachers / Faculty

Operates through a lean teaching layer with batch context, attendance, tests, and intervention signals.

Sees Timetable, roster, attendance history, test readiness, weak students
Acts On Teaching, attendance marking, score entry, remedial action
Business Value Better academic consistency and quicker intervention
Operations

Academic Coordinator

Keeps the academic engine synchronized across rooms, batches, faculty, and exam flow.

Sees Room allocation, batch map, faculty load, test calendar, substitution risk
Acts On Scheduling, substitutions, capacity balancing, daily escalations
Business Value Smoother day-to-day execution with fewer disruptions
Support

Front Desk / Admin

Handles the human side of operations: walk-ins, documents, queries, updates, and coordination.

Sees Visitor logs, pending documents, notice boards, parent requests
Acts On Registration, support closure, data updates, communication routing
Business Value Reduced process friction and cleaner front-office flow

Learner Surface

Students

Students get a discipline-first experience built around clarity, routine, and measurable progress.

  • Timetable and attendance view
  • Homework, tests, and result visibility
  • Doubt session updates and notices
  • Self-accountability loop

Guardian Surface

Parents

Parents see the trust layer: attendance, fees, results, alerts, and communication without chasing the institute.

  • Attendance and absentee alerts
  • Fees, receipts, and payment reminders
  • Result trends and academic visibility
  • Two-way communication channel

Scale Surface

Branch Heads

Branch leadership sees local performance with enough authority to move quickly while staying aligned centrally.

  • Branch dashboard and occupancy view
  • Collections and local admissions performance
  • Faculty and room utilization
  • Regional comparison and governance
